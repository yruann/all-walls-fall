ALL WALLS FALL presents twelve artists whose works unearth, distort, and reanimate fragmented worlds—the internal crawling out, the buzzing of creation exposed to the light. Volatile and playful. Using only what can be built, salvaged, or repurposed, each piece reveals the hidden systems of making, the cycle of deteriorating life consumed again and again to be reborn. Walls crack, wires buzz, wood creaks. And when art is expected to exist easily, this show exposes those rusty foundations. Unsteady negotiations were made. Works teeter back and forth. Walls crack and crumble. These structures begin their journey to obsoletion. The microscopic, the hidden are there instead. Yet, there’s an intimacy in the decay, in the path to nothingness. Decay. It gives you nothingness—as much as you desire.
